# Innovation balances drive with rethinking

Successful innovation requires choosing the right problem to solve, and doing so with unwavering certainty, balanced by an active desire to avoid confusing assumptions with reality.

> ==Innovation is powered by the combination of **intensity** and a **willingness to reconsider assumptions**==, minimizing the chance of following dead ends and maximizing the potential for finding better paths.[^1]
